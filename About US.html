About Us

Mod movie theater is a recognized leader in the entertainment industry as one of the top movie theaters in Botswana.


History

Under the leadership of Artistic Director Modimoosi Kgosiemang since 1998, Mod movie Theater is a nationally recognized theatre, presenting an acclaimed mainstage season while providing a wide variety of programming and experiences for Batswana. Founded in 1990, the theater has been the largest distributor of animated, faith-based and actioned packed content. Mod movie theatres is also at the forefront of bringing anime, performing arts, and classic film to moviegoers worldwide.

Audiences can count on Mod to curate and deliver exclusive entertainment at their local movie theater, giving fans of any genre the opportunity to share in unique and memorable experiences on the big screen. Mod audiences enjoy exclusive bonus content.

Mods’s nationwide network of connected cinemas can be leveraged for private movie screenings, companywide townhalls, fan activations, product launches, and more. These exclusive cinema-based programs offer customized content delivered prerecorded or live by satellite to a targeted footprint of local theaters.


Network

Mod is the number one largest distributor of content to movie theaters in Botswana. The company ranks alongside Hollywood’s major studios, with limited screening dates and showtimes. Mod operates the largest cinema distribution network with over 1,100 participating locations and nearly 2,000 screens in 184 DMAs (including all of the top 100 markets), which continues to grow double-digits year-over-year. Mod also has a global presence, distributing content (28 titles in 2019) internationally to more than 45 countries and over 2,100 screens worldwide.
